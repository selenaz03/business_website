Because no one should go hungry.

What We Do

Our mission is to advance change in America by ensuring equitable access to nutritious food for all in partnership with food banks, policymakers, supporters, and the communities we serve. We believe that no one in America should go hungry, no matter their socioeconomic status. Everyone deserves to eat.

An America where no one is hungry.
In a country that wastes billions of pounds of food each year, it's almost shocking that anyone in America goes hungry.
Yet every day, millions of our neighbors don't get the food they need to live healthy lives.
We work to get nourishing food – from farmers, manufacturers, and retailers – to our neighbors while addressing the root causes of hunger.
